About Us

Expense Manager: An Online Solution to your money management and accounting needs.
An initiative brought to reality by the hard efforts of Ashish Sharma, Ankit Agrawal and Nikhil Vyas.

From Ceo's Desk:

Expense Manager basic idea is to provide a platform for user to manage their account and business related
transaction with ease and efficiency.

Services

EXPENSE MANAGER provides a group of broad functionality such as

  1. It Provides user login and sign up facility
  2. It manages user expenses based on the form daily inputs   
  3. It creates the Charts and graphs related to the monthly expenses as well as routine purchases.   
  4. It gives users an estimate of their monthly / avergae expenses and allows to keep a track on it
  5. It allows the user to keep a track on their budgets and company expenses.   
  6. Last but not the least it build around an easy to use environment to claculate and manage the cost.
