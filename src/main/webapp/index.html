Here's what EQC is

EQC is an application of Natural Language Processing which refers to the use and ability of computer systems to understand and process chunks of lemma belonging to a natural language such as English. Relation establishment is a key NLP technique for exploring significant relations that would be useful for information retrieval, classification and summarization. EQC is a novel approach of applying NLP to Academia for analysis of academic evaluatory techniques primarily competitive written examinations.
